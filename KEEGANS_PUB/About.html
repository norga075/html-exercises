ABOUT

Keegan’s Irish Pub was designed and built by O’Sullivan Interiors in Waterford, Ireland. The design was completed in late 2001 and the pub was built in Waterford then transported via container ship and train in January, 2002.

We take great pride in our offerings, especially our perfect pints. Keegan’s Pub is honored year after year by Guinness for the way we pour and serve our beer. All our Guinness comes from the St. James Gate brewery in Dublin, Ireland and we pour it at the right temperature with right nitrogen to CO2 mixture.
